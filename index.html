Campus Life

school church

Was established in 1978

The school church (established in 1978) is a place where we pray to God for our hopes and wishes to come true. We also make students sit here for just 5 minutes at the start of the year to pray for a god year.

school library

Was built in 1980

The school library is a place where we can all have some quiet time reading books and relaxing. The school library took 5 years to build as next to it is a classroom which doubled the building time.

school auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school auditorium is a place where we sing and do our concerts. The auditorium was built in 1982 for the purpose of a timeout corner, but we thought it would be best if we played our concerts there./p>

school achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

In 2015, we won the school debate on enviroment pollution. 15 schools went up and declared what to do for the pollution that's happening in this world. In the end, our school won 1st place and won 3.2 million dollars.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

In 1999, 17 schools went head to head in a football competition. This happened because we wanted to see who was the best school for football. Our school came 2nd place with 1st place being Banyan Fields Primary School.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second price in inter school Science Project competition

In 2002, 5 schools competed in a science project fair because the goverment wanted to see which 3 schools could help them study plants. In the end, our school came 2nd with the winner being Berwick Fields Primary School.
